We work with you,
not for you.

Parallel Labs started in 2014 with the goal of bridging the gap between design, technology and business. Designers and developers were working in silos, there was lack of collaboration and ultimately failing to meet business goals.

The idea was to create an integrated approach to digital product design. We consciously created a diverse team with self taught designers, hustlers, startup enthusiasts and developers passionate about design which helps us connect with startups.

Clients approach us at different stages of their product and business journey. Sometimes they have an idea and help build a MLP, whereas sometimes they need a full revamp or partner with us to continuously drive their product from a design perspective.

1 We are a Startup at Heart

You won’t have to explain to us what things like Bootstrapping and MVP mean. We love playing Devil’s advocate, challenging your assumptions just enough that you still like us. And don’t be surprised if we know more about your competitors than you do.

2 We are not an Agency

We neither pitch nor sell. We will work as your extended team and make sure we understand your business inside-out. All of our UX is done in-house because that’s what works. We partner with teams we believe in and they in us.

3 We are pragmatic, lean and fast

We know what it takes to build good product. Our processes are designed to allow clients to ship, launch, test and iterate. Our model relies on just enough research and plenty of business and market insights. All of this, fast.

4 We walk along till the Last Mile

Our job doesn’t end at providing wireframes or design files. Whether it’s sharing production ready Front-end code instead of PSDs or testing products while under development, we do everything it takes to have the experience be exactly as we envisioned it.
