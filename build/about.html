We don’t work for you,
We work with you.

Parallel Labs started in 2014 with the goal of bridging the gap between design, technology and business. Designers and developers were working in silos, there was lack of collaboration thus products failed in meeting business goals.

The idea was to create an integrated approach to digital product design. We consciously created a diverse team with self taught designers, hustlers, startup enthusiasts and developers passionate about design.

Clients approach us at different stages of their product and business journey. Sometimes they have an idea and we help build a MLP, whereas sometimes they need a full revamp ( and we partner with them to ) continuously drive their product from a design perspective.

1 We work on projects that matter

Solving problems that makes a difference to people’s lives gives us a kick. We don’t work on everything that comes across us but only on projects that make a difference to our lives.

2 We are a Startup at Heart

You won’t have to explain to us what things like Bootstrapping and MVP mean. We love playing Devil’s advocate, challenging your assumptions just enough that you still like us. And don’t be surprised if we know more about your competitors than you do.

3 We are not an Agency

We neither pitch nor sell. We will work as your extended team and make sure we understand your business inside-out. All of our UX is done in-house because that’s what works. We partner with teams we believe in.

4 We are pragmatic, lean and fast

We know what it takes to build good products. Our processes are designed to allow clients to ship, launch, test and iterate. Our model relies on just enough research and plenty of business and market insights. All of this, fast.
