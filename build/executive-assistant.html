Executive Assistant Full Time     .     Bangalore Apply

Give us Wings with your skills to get things done

We are looking for someone who:

  • Loves what we do, you should have an artist somewhere hidden inside
  • Loves Startups and knows about new launches, funding and shut downs
  • Good articulation skills
  • Prioritises craft & learning over perks

Must Have:

  • Strong organizational skills. You should be the kind of person who never lets a ball drop, but flexible when asked to let a ball drop
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills. Should know how to write clear and professional emails
  • A love for creating efficient systems and checklists
  • Excitement about working at a startup and the work we do
  • Warmth, resourcefulness and good judgement. Your friends should sometimes describe you as “sensible.”

Responsibilities

  • Take care of recurring office maintenance: internet connections, prints and scans, calling the handyman etc
  • Recruiting coordination, sourcing, trip planning and other special projects
  • Handle day-to-day office errands like couriers
  • Drafting proposals, contracts, PPTs and employee letters/documents based on existing templates
  • Managing online presence on official social media platforms
  • Keep our office well stocked with food, snacks, drinks, and supplies. Research, purchase, maintain office equipment

What will you get:

  • Diverse team with Business, Design and Technical Skills, we are not a herd
  • Office that provides space to think, do and unwind. Lots of sunlight, amazing view, X-box and relaxed space.
  • Pantry full of snacks and soft drinks. We also make our own Cappuccino with a lovely coffee maker :-)
  • Balanced work environment and flexibility in timings

Career Prospects

  • You will be like the right hand to CEO in this role and assisting tasks cutting across verticals which will give you immense exposure into different work areas. Going forward depending on your interests and skills, you will have the option to switch to a specific role like HR, Management or even design.
