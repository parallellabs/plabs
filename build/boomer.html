Boomer Marketing

Helping small businesses market like a pro.

Get it on Google Play

Boomer has been the most challenging project we have worked on at Parallel Labs. It’s a super powerful app which helps Small Businesses manage their complete marketing from a mobile app.

  • Build and manage a responsive website.
  • Chat live with users and recieve IP based calls.
  • Manage and push updates to all social channels in one tap.
  • Launch promotions like offers and events from the app.

Onboarding a new Business

Compose Screens

Compose screens for creating different types of content which can be pushed to website as well in one click. Since small businesses generally don’t have resources to create good content, we came up with a idea to integrate google and Flickr images in compose screens allowing users to fetch royalty free images instantly from these platforms and make content engaging and presentable.

Switching Business & Manager

We considered a completely different approach to navigation to facilitate two key requirements allowing a user to switch between different businesses he owns and also switch the active manager in one click who will receive all calls and get notifications for enquiries currently.
