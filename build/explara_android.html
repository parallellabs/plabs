Local experiences on the go.

Get it on Google Play

With a shift in focus to local experiences, having a mobile app with a well thought of User Experience was the new strategy for Explara.

We worked together with Explara team to design first version of their mobile app using Material Design guidelines. Overall focus was to keep User Experience really simple and skip advanced features and navigation options as it was a new segment and a simple experience was key to introduce it to users.

Walkthroughs

For an application that promises to make our life more fun with interesting experiences, it was important to get them excited about it with walkthroughs. Hence, we chose to make them fun with a interactive approach.

FAB

We decided to use the Floating Action Button to create a alternate browsing flow with a approach “What do you want to do today ?” and make it fun.

Event Details

Interactive mock of Event Details Screen with a clean approach to listing and help event stand out.
