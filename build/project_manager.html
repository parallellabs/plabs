Product Manager Apply

We're looking for someone to join our talented team as a Product Manager. You'll work closely with clients, designers, developers, and the rest of the team to help them build great products, on schedule.

What are we looking for:

  • You are organized, pay attention to detail and have opinions on best ways to solve a problem.
  • Ability to plan projects and work with a cross-functional team to determine timelines, estimates, requirements, and scope, as well as track progress, mitigate issues and risks, and maintain standards.
  • An eye for good design, understanding of technology, knowledge best practices and terminologies of UX design/Android/iOS.
  • Has strong written & verbal communication skills, as well as ability to build healthy relationships with clients & team alike.
  • Should be able to get things done without being arrogant. Essentially be a manager whom both team and clients love.
  • Ability to put ideas to thought by means of creating sketches, wireframes, user stories etc.

What will you get:

  • Working with a talented team bunch of people who are really passionate about their craft
  • A office that provides space to think, do and unwind.
  • Open minded atmosphere with freedom to experiment, bringing in new ideas and be like a team on purpose.
  • Feel home with our pantry loaded with snacks/drinks and a game zone with Xbox One.
