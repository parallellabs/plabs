Digital gifting is still at a very nascent stage in India and that’s very Treatr saw an opportunity to create a product which makes sending and redeeming gift cards a breeze as well as fun.

Getting Real - Redefining the Product to make it simple and practical

When we came into picture, Treatr team already had very detailed wireframes of the entire workflow with tons of features and they were looking to build an MVP that too with a dev partner.

Although it wasn’t what were hired for, like always we ended up questioning why behind the product architecture and over a series of product strategy workshops, helped redefine the product making it more simple by removing what was not needed an MVP and bringing better ways of on-board and card redemption.

Material Principle and not guidelines

With Google’s Material design becoming mainstream, it witnessed a mass adoption but soon all applications started looking same as designers were using the reference examples shared in Google’s design language. But material design is a design system which if applied intelligently can create interesting and distinct applications. Hence we decided to experiment with using material design principles and extended it with our own imagination.

Illustrated Walkthroughs to evoke expression of sending gift

Gifting is an emotive experience and the application needed to bring that for new users on-boarding on the app. Hence, we developed the squirrel used in the Treatr logo as a mascot and extended it further to create illustrated walkthroughs explaining features of the application.

Simple and sleek on-boarding

Although extremely simple and clean, we made these on-boarding screens look sleek with the right combination of colors, layout and typography.

Allowing redemption of cards instantly on phone without having to download app

To make redemption of gift cards easy and at the same time keeping it secure, we create a mobile web workflow where users can redeem their cards by just entering a OTP sent to their mobile phones.

Personalizing gifts with picture and a voice message

To make the gift more personal and emotive, we create a personalise experience comprising of ability add a picture and a voice message along with the gift.
