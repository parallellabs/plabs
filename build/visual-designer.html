Craft digital experiences that look as good as they work.

We are looking for someone who:

  • Gets bored often working on the same stuff, and needs fresh challenges to keep the juices flowing.
  • Hates the status Quo and needs freedom to experiment and try bold designs
  • Is a Quick Learner and has a perfect balance of left & right brain
  • Prioritizes craft over perks

Must Have:

  • A pantry that’s always stacked with food. We also make our own Cappuccino with a lovely coffee maker :)
  • Balanced work environment and flexibility in timings
  • A macbook, high end monitors and latest software/tools.
