Interface Designer Full Time     .     Bangalore Apply

We are looking for 1-4 years experienced interface designer to join our team. Your degree and certifications don't matter, what matters is your craft. This job is for you if you are someone:

  • Who gets bored working on the same stuff continously and wants something new & challenging every 2 months
  • Who needs the creative freedom to experiment different styles and bold designs
  • Who wants to own product design rather be part of a 30 people team designing an app
  • For whom craft comes first and perks come later

What are we looking for:

  • Understanding of the digital media nuances like Responsive Design, grids, Android/iOS conventions, material design guidelines etc.
  • Good aesthetic sense and understanding of design fundamentals (Doesn’t matter if you went to a design school or not)
  • Experience in practising User Experience Design and obsession with design detail.
  • Have an Attitude of continuous improvement. You should never hesitate to junk what you have done and redo for better.
  • Understanding of fundamentals of Web technologies like HTML/CSS3 (Don’t worry, we don’t expect you to be a unicorn and program complex stuff and you will be designing apps/websites but its important to know the medium well)
  • Hands on experience with design and mockup tools like Photoshop/Sketch. Experience with animation tools like AfterEffects, Quartz, Composer and Framer will be a bonus.

What will you get:

  • Get to work with a team with diversified skills on Visual Design, Product Design and Front-end Development.
  • Work on exciting consumer facing projects that make an impact
  • Office that provides space to think, do and unwind. We have designed our new office (we are shifting there in June) keeping in mind all of these. Whether its our Lounge area with Xbox One for fun and a huge whiteboard wall with a screen for brainstorming sessions or high rise seating with a beautiful view, its all to give that environment to do awesome work.
  • Freedom to experiment and innovate and have your say.
  • Pantry full of snacks and soft drinks. Call us orthodox but we don't get alcohol to work :-)
  • Balanced work enviroment and flexibility in timings
